# World Authority Profiles

## SOLO_WORLD
- Single authority actor.
- Offline-first continuity ownership.
- Local deterministic continuity resolution.

## FAMILY_WORLD
- Delegated authority with inheritance.
- Shared replay lineage with trust boundaries.
- Authority transfer recorded as canonical events.

## COMMUNITY_WORLD
- Moderation authority governance.
- Plugin legality enforcement for shared worlds.
- Replay attestation required for canonical merges.

## FEDERATED_WORLD
- Distributed authority domains.
- Cross-domain continuity reconciliation.
- Replay verification before federation commit.
