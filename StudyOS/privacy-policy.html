Privacy Policy — StudyOS

Last updated: 2026-03-16

StudyOS is designed to help users study, plan, and track progress. This policy explains what data may be stored, how it is used, and what control users have.

1. Data we process

  • Study session and planner data entered by the user.
  • Progress metrics generated from user activity in the app.
  • Optional diagnostic metadata for reliability and feature quality.

2. How data is used

  • To provide core study planning and progress tracking features.
  • To sync and restore user state across supported sessions/devices (if enabled).
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

3. Data sharing

StudyOS does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers required to operate the app, subject to contractual safeguards.

4. Retention and deletion

Data is retained only as long as needed for app functionality, legal obligations, and operational integrity. Users may request deletion of account-linked data where applicable.

5. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational controls are used to protect data; however, no internet-based system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Children

StudyOS is not intended for unsupervised use by children under the age required by local law without parental consent.

7. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact: privacy@via-decide.com.
