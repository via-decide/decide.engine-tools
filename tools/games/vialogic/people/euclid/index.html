ACT I: THE HISTORY

The Ghost of Alexandria

c. 300 BC

We know almost nothing about his life. No birthplace, no death date, no physical description. Yet, there is a famous historical quote: 'Euclid means Mathematics, and Mathematics means Euclid.'

HISTORICAL INSIGHT: His personal identity vanished, entirely absorbed by the absolute perfection of his work.
