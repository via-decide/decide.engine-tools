# Living Archive

Living Archive preserves computational identity evolution through replayable history, lineage tracking, emotional notes, and rediscovery.

## Modules
- Archive persistence and artifact indexing
- Lineage graph and timeline grouping
- Replay history integration with replay-engine
- Search by tags/constraints/notes
- Discovery mode for resurfacing forgotten experiments

## Principle
No ranking or scoring systems. Preserve weirdness, accidents, and emotional traces.
